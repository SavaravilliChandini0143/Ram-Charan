Ram Charan

His Biography:

Ram Charan K, also known as Ram Charan, is a prominent Indian actor and entrepreneur primarily known for his work in Telugu cinema. He was born on March 27, 1985, in Chennai, India, to famous actor Chiranjeevi and Surekha. Ram Charan made his acting debut with the film "Chirutha" in 2007, which earned him the Filmfare Award for Best Male Debut. He rose to fame with successful films like "Magadheera" (2009), "Racha" (2012), "Naayak" (2013), and "Dhruva" (2016). He has also made a mark with his performances in the action-packed and emotional roles, gaining recognition for his versatility. Apart from acting, Ram Charan is involved in various business ventures, including being a co-owner of the Hyderabad Polo and Riding Club and part of the film production company Konidela Production Company. Ram Charan also starred in the highly anticipated film "RRR" (2022), directed by S.S. Rajamouli, where his performance was widely praised. He is married to Upasana Kamineni, and he continues to be a major figure in Indian cinema with a growing fanbase across the globe.
